Loan Programs

Find the right fit, in plain English

Best for: strong credit

Conventional

3–20% down. Most flexible, often cheapest long-term.

Best for: first-timers

FHA

As little as 3.5% down, more flexible credit requirements.

Best for: veterans

VA

0% down, no monthly mortgage insurance, for veterans & active duty.

Best for: rural/suburban

USDA

0% down for buyers in eligible areas around Greater Houston.

Best for: higher price points

Jumbo

Loan amounts above conforming limits, competitive rates and terms.

Best for: current owners

Refinance / Cash-Out

Lower your rate or tap equity for renovations or debt payoff.
